Telephone Bell

 

Tested with Chrome 58.0.3029.110 (64-bit)

Telephone Bell

Interface comments

  • Pedestrian Crossing is missing on the pop-up menu (once you are into a specific sound synthesis)

  • Clock is missing on the pop-up menu (once you are into a specific sound synthesis)

  • Add number boxes to display slider values with relative unit of measure (e.g. Hz, seconds)

  • "Bell 1 Pitch" - change "pitch" to "frequency" to be consistents with taxonomy used in the other sounds

 

Model comments

  • Timing issue: bells should be triggered much faster , triggering frequency should be between 8 and 10 Hz

  • The synthesis in itself sounds good (the distribution and amplitude of partials of single bells are similar to selected sample)

  • Add an impulsive noisy component to each bell sound (simulation of hammer beating the bell)?

 

Sound designers comments

  • Not bad, but the bell type could have some more choices as it may pass for an older style phone bell, but not the more modern ones IMHO

  • The presets were a little sluggish to operate (browser related maybe)

  • The parameters only produce usable sounds in very specific sweet-spots
