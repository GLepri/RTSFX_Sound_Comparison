RTSFX review

 

Comparing RTSFX sounds with high quality samples

Here you can find a systematic comparison between the RTSFX sounds with high quality samples.
High quality sounds representative of the sonic taxonomies present in the RTSFX index have been selected.
The comparison is carried out using scientific tools for the representation and analysis of audio data (e.g. Sonic Visualiser).
In order to highligt differencies, similarities and potential improovements, for each analysed sound, a few comments are provided.
Furthermore, while comparing the various sonic materials it is was possible to individiuate those RTSFX settings associated to the most accurate reproduction of the various samples considered.
This process therefore helps to define RTSFX pre-sets, for each sound a link to related RTSFX settings is therefore provided.
Not all the RTSFX sounds are here evaluated, the focus is on those sounds that, according to the author viewpoint, needs to be significantly improoved.
